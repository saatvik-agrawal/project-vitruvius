World Café Workshop

The World Café workshop is designed to facilitate structured yet flexible conversations in a relaxed setting. Participants engage in rotating small-group discussions to generate creative solutions and insights on key topics.

What You’ll Need

  • Several small tables with 4-5 chairs each
  • Large sheets of paper or tablecloths for writing
  • Markers or pens for participants
  • Snacks or drinks to create a welcoming atmosphere
  • A facilitator to guide the process

Steps

  1. Introduction: Explain the objectives of the World Café and introduce the discussion topic.
  2. First Round of Discussions: Participants are seated at tables and begin discussing the topic while taking notes.
  3. Table Rotation: After a set time (15-20 minutes), participants rotate to a new table, leaving one person behind as a "table host" to summarize previous discussions.
  4. Building on Ideas: New participants contribute fresh insights to the discussion, refining and expanding on previous points.
  5. Final Round and Synthesis: After multiple rotations, participants review the collected insights and identify common themes.
  6. Reflection: The facilitator leads a closing discussion to summarize key takeaways and next steps.

Best Practices

  • Encourage a relaxed and open atmosphere for free-flowing discussions.
  • Ensure participants respect all ideas and viewpoints.
  • Use a visual summary at the end to capture key themes and ideas.
  • Allow participants to record insights using both writing and drawing.
