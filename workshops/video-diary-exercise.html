Video Diary Exercise Workshop

This workshop encourages participants to document and reflect on their experiences through video diaries. By recording their thoughts and observations, participants gain deeper insights into their surroundings and personal reflections.

What Youâ€™ll Need

  • Diaries or notebooks for planning
  • Smartphones, cameras, or other video recording devices
  • Access to a video editing tool (optional)
  • A shared space for reviewing and discussing recorded content
  • Facilitator to provide guidance and structure

Steps

  1. Introduction: Explain the objectives of the video diary exercise and its value in self-reflection and storytelling.
  2. Planning: Participants define their themes and outline what they wish to capture in their video diaries.
  3. Recording: Participants record short videos reflecting on their observations, emotions, or daily experiences.
  4. Editing (Optional): If time allows, participants can edit their footage to enhance storytelling.
  5. Sharing and Discussion: Participants share selected video clips and discuss common themes and insights.
  6. Reflection: Wrap up with a discussion on how video diaries can be used for personal development, research, or community engagement.

Best Practices

  • Encourage authenticity and spontaneity in video recordings.
  • Ensure a safe and respectful environment for sharing personal reflections.
  • Provide basic filming and editing tips to enhance video quality.
  • Document and archive videos for future learning or storytelling projects.
